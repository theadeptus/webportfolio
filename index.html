About me

I provide front-end web development services for professionals, organizations, and private companies.

Professional Background

I'm an industrial engineering graduate. I first worked as a call center agent before moving to a finished goods warehouse as an operations specialist and then to a courier warehouse as a coordinator--two positions which are more in line with my college degree. When the pandemic happened in 2020 the warehouse had to close for several weeks and stopped their operations temporarily. During the downtime with the possibility of being laid off and for personal reasons, I decided it was better for me to move to a different work so I tried applying for a government job since I passed the Civil Service Commission Professional Exam. I worked as an administrative assistant under a contract which ended last December 2021. I now want to pursue my interest in web development and I am fortunate to have found Zuitt where I can learn about it and get a job in the field.

Why I Chose Web Development

I remember as a teenager that I was customizing themes on my blog which sparked an interest in wanting to go for the web dev field, but due to circumstances it was kept in the backburner of my life for many years. Long story short, I wanted the flexibility of the work and I find it fun to build and customize websites. Nowadays as we are transitioning more and more into the digitalization of most products and services, being in the web dev field will prove to be lucrative.
